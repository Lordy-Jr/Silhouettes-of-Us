Silhouettes of Us

And I will kiss every scar on your body,

trace the echoes of your battles with my lips,

and rewrite pain into poetry.

Let me love you past the ghosts of yesterday,

past the shadows that still linger in your gaze.

I will hold you in a silence between heartbeats,

where the world forgets its cruelty,

where only we exist, undone and infinite.

And if the night remembers your sorrow,

I will unmake the darkness.

If the past clings to your skin,

I will shatter time itself just to pull you free.

You are the storm and the sky,

the fire and the calm,

and if there is even a fragment of ache left within you,

I will love you until it surrenders.

Forever yours ❤️
Carrots
